How is Kipchoge Score calculated?

Eliud Kipchoge set the marathon WR of 2:00:35 at Berlin 2022. Average pace: 2:51 /km (5.83 m/s).

Your score = your marathon speed (BMO 2025 chip: 5:42:46) ÷ Kipchoge's WR speed × 100. Marathon vs marathon — the fairest comparison.
